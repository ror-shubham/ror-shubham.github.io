My journey at 6sense spanned nearly five years, beginning shortly after the Covid lockdowns and evolving from building frontend architectures from scratch to owning massive, AI-driven data pipelines.

Below is a breakdown of the core systems and platforms I engineered during my tenure.

AI Email Writer & Account Summary#

In my most recent initiatives, I focused heavily on Generative AI infrastructure:

  • AI Email Writer: Built an LLM generation system processing 1,500+ emails daily using intent data, technographics, CRM history, and behavioral signals. This achieved a 42% reply rate and a 29% increase in booked meetings. I implemented multi-layer guardrails, a credit-based usage system with org-level quotas, and owned the prompt design.
  • AI Account Summary: Engineered an engine generating 20,000+ daily summaries covering activity recaps, buying stage predictions, and intent insights. Adding response caching and async processing significantly reduced redundant LLM calls and latency, driving an 11% increase in Stage 0 pipeline.

Sales Intelligence Platform#

Following the acquisition of Slintel, I was part of the 3-member architect team responsible for merging the legacy 6sense Sales Intelligence with Slintel’s technology.

  • We executed a total UI overhaul and established a new design language, creating the boilerplate to connect with our backend and porting reusable frontend components.
  • The 5-month project involved 15 engineers and ultimately resulted in a highly successful unified app generating $20M+ in revenue.

Sales Dashboards#

My foundational project at 6sense was creating the Sales Dashboards app from scratch.

  • Acting as the sole frontend developer initially, I defined the frontend architecture (JavaScript + React + GraphQL + Apollo), set up the CI/CD pipeline, and built the core reusable component library.
  • The app served as the starting point for the sales team to get insights and recommendations until its deprecation in March 2025.

SI Alerts & Data Pipelines#

A massive component of my role involved backend data processing and automated alerting:

  • SI Alerts: Developed and owned the full stack for sending 90,000+ custom intent alerts daily. The backend was powered by Python and PySpark, utilizing Jinja2 to generate robust email templates optimized for notorious native clients like Microsoft Outlook.
  • Tech Team Leadership: As part of the 6-member SI Tech pod, I handled PagerDuty, SLA compliance, and data pipeline maintenance (using Hive, Hadoop, and PySpark). I successfully optimized a critical data pipeline DAG, reducing daily run time by over 45 minutes, and achieved $130,000 in annualized savings by decommissioning legacy databases.

Salesloft iFrame Integration#

To ensure the Sales Intelligence app was accessible wherever our users worked, I engineered the iFrame integration for supported CRMs.

  • I created and maintained the backend APIs (Django, GraphQL) to seamlessly support the integration specifically for Salesloft, managing its lifecycle from 2022 onwards.
