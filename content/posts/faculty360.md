**[Faculty360](https://web.archive.org/web/20190626102938/https://www.faculty360.com/)** is a platform designed to streamline the process of faculty recruitment and management for educational institutions.

## What I Built

I developed the frontend for the platform, creating a responsive interface that enabled:

- Faculty profile management and discovery
- Job posting and application workflows for institutions
- Search and filter functionality across faculty databases
- Dashboard views for both institutions and candidates

## Technologies Used

`HTML` · `CSS` · `Bootstrap` · `JavaScript`
