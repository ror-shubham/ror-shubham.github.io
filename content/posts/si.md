- 6sense acquired a company named Slintel. Slintel and legacy 6sense Sales Intelligence were merged to form new Sales 
Intelligence app I was part of 3 member architect team responsible for taking technology decisions which included 
creating boilerplate to connect with our backend, switch to typescript from javascript, using craco framework, porting
reusable front components from legacy app.
- The ideation started with the problem statement of merging the two apps. We started thinking of just adding one
app's Frontend as an iframe in the other app, but instead we ended up with a total UI overhaul, complete new design
language and major changes in architecture.
- This was a big project which took 5 months and 15 engineers and a lot of late nights.
- The app was a huge success bringing in 20 million+ revenue
